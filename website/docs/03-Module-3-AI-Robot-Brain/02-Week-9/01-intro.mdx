## 1. What is SLAM?

**SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping)** is the chicken-and-egg problem:
1.  To map the room, I need to know where I am.
2.  To know where I am, I need a map of the room.

## 2. Visual SLAM (vSLAM)

Using cameras (RGB-D or Stereo) to extract feature points from the environment.
*   **Loop Closure**: Recognizing a place we've been before to correct drift.
*   **RTAB-Map**: Real-Time Appearance-Based Mapping.
